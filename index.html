About Us

Established in 2009, Classic Bearings is a well-established industrial supplier specializing in high-quality bearings and power transmission products. We cater to manufacturing units, workshops, and engineering industries across Bangalore and nearby industrial areas.

As an Authorized Distributor for PIX V-Belts and a trusted stockist of leading brands such as SKF, NTN, FAG, and NBC, we ensure only genuine products, consistent availability, and competitive pricing.

With a strong focus on reliability, timely supply, and long-term customer relationships, Classic Bearings has earned the trust of multiple industrial clients who depend on us for smooth and uninterrupted operations.
